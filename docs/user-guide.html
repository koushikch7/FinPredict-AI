📘 User Guide

Step-by-step instructions for getting the most out of FinPredict AI.

🚀 Getting Started

1Create Your Account

After launching FinPredict AI, navigate to the login page and click "Register":

  • Choose a username (3-20 characters, alphanumeric)
  • Enter a valid email address
  • Create a strong password (8+ characters, mix of letters/numbers)

You'll be automatically logged in after registration.

2Configure AI Provider

Go to Settings → AI Configuration and add at least one provider:

  • Gemini: Free tier with 15 RPM. Get key from Google AI Studio
  • OpenAI: Paid API. Get key from OpenAI Dashboard
  • Arbiter: Recommended! Self-hosted gateway with 11+ providers. See Arbiter docs
💡 Tip: Arbiter is the best choice — it handles provider rotation, fallback, and rate limiting automatically.

3Build Your Watchlist

Navigate to Stocks and search for symbols to track:

  • Search by company name (e.g., "Reliance") or symbol (e.g., "RELIANCE.NS")
  • Click the + button to add to your watchlist
  • The app comes pre-seeded with 166 popular NSE/BSE stocks

📊 Using the Dashboard

Portfolio Overview

The dashboard shows a real-time snapshot of your paper trading portfolio:

  • Total Value: Cash + current value of holdings
  • P&L: Total profit/loss with percentage
  • Top Movers: Your holdings with biggest daily changes
  • Recent Predictions: Latest AI signals for your watchlist

Market Overview

Quick view of market indices and sector performance:

  • Nifty 50, Bank Nifty, Sensex
  • Sector-wise gainers/losers
  • Market breadth (advances vs declines)

🤖 Getting AI Predictions

1View Existing Predictions

Click on any stock in your watchlist to see its latest prediction:

  • Signal: BUY, SELL, or HOLD
  • Conviction: 0-100% confidence score
  • Targets: Short, medium, and long-term price targets
  • Stop Loss: Recommended exit if trade goes wrong

2Generate Fresh Prediction

Click the 🔄 Refresh button to generate a new prediction:

  • Latest market data is fetched
  • Technical indicators recalculated
  • News sentiment re-analyzed
  • AI generates fresh signal with reasoning
⚠️ Rate limits: Free AI tiers have limits. Gemini: 15 RPM, OpenAI: varies by plan.

3Choose Strategy

Select a strategy that matches your investment style:

  • Buffett: Warren Buffett-style, quality companies with moats
  • Lynch: Peter Lynch-style, growth stocks with strong momentum
  • Graham: Benjamin Graham-style, deep value with margin of safety
  • Momentum: For trending markets, follows strong price moves
  • MeanReversion: Contrarian, bets on oversold bounces
  • Balanced: Weighted mix of all strategies for stability

📈 Paper Trading

Executing Trades

Practice trading without risking real money:

  1. Go to Paper Trading or click Trade on any stock
  2. Enter quantity (in shares)
  3. Choose order type: Market (execute now) or Limit (set price)
  4. Review estimated cost including brokerage
  5. Click Buy or Sell

Understanding Charges

Paper trading simulates real Zerodha charges:

  • Brokerage: 0.03% or ₹20 per order (whichever is lower)
  • STT: 0.1% on sell value
  • Exchange fees: 0.00345%
  • GST: 18% on brokerage
  • SEBI charges: ₹10 per crore

Tracking Performance

Monitor your paper trading results in Paper Trading → Performance:

  • Win Rate: Percentage of profitable trades
  • Average P&L: Mean profit/loss per trade
  • Best/Worst Trade: Extremes of your trading
  • Sharpe Ratio: Risk-adjusted returns

💬 AI Chat Assistant

What You Can Ask

The AI assistant knows your portfolio and market context:

  • "What's the outlook for TCS this week?"
  • "Should I add more Reliance to my portfolio?"
  • "Find me oversold banking stocks"
  • "Analyze my paper trading performance"
  • "What's driving Tata Motors today?"
  • "Compare HDFC Bank vs ICICI Bank"

Tips for Better Responses

  • Be specific: "INFY" instead of "Infosys stock"
  • Mention timeframe: "next week" or "for swing trading"
  • Ask one question at a time for detailed answers

🔗 Connecting Zerodha

1Get Kite API Access

Apply for Kite Connect API at kite.trade:

  • ₹2,000 one-time + ₹2,000/month subscription
  • Approval takes 1-2 business days
  • You'll receive API key and secret

2Configure in FinPredict

Go to Settings → Broker Integration:

  • Enter your Kite API Key
  • Enter your Kite API Secret
  • Click Connect
  • You'll be redirected to Zerodha login

3Sync Holdings

After connecting:

  • Your real holdings are imported automatically
  • Compare paper vs real portfolio performance
  • Holdings refresh daily at market open
🔒 Security: FinPredict only has read access. It cannot execute trades on your real account.

🔍 Using the Scanner

Pre-built Scans

Find opportunities quickly with preset filters:

  • Momentum Breakouts: Stocks breaking above resistance with volume
  • Oversold Bounces: RSI below 30, showing reversal signs
  • Volume Spikes: 2x+ average volume today
  • 52-Week Highs: Stocks at all-time highs
  • 52-Week Lows: Potential value plays at lows

Custom Scans

Build your own filter criteria:

  • Price range, market cap, sector
  • RSI, MACD, volume thresholds
  • P/E, P/B, dividend yield ranges
  • Combine multiple conditions with AND/OR
